1. Stencil Art
(Hand-drawn)

Stencil art is a versatile and impactful art form where artists use pre-cut templates to apply paint, ink, or other mediums, creating sharp, repeatable designs on various surfaces. This technique is employed in a range of styles from street art and graffiti to fine art and decorative patterns. Stencil art's precision and ability to produce bold, clean lines make it ideal for both detailed illustrations and large-scale public artworks.

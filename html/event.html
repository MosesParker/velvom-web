Our Events

Famous Events

1. ITC Chennai

Recently, we attended a delightful painting event at ITC Chennai, where we continuously drew caricatures and portraits of people, including children. The event was filled with laughter and joy as participants eagerly posed for their unique, personalized artwork. This engaging and heartwarming activity not only showcased artistic talent but also created cherished memories and brought smiles to everyone involved.

Location: Chennai


2. Karumegangal Kalaigindrana Audio Launch

We participated in the Karumegangal Kalaigindrana movie audio launch event, where we drew caricatures and portraits of film celebrities. The event was vibrant and filled with excitement as we captured the unique personalities of the stars in our artwork. This engaging activity added a touch of fun and creativity, bringing smiles and creating memorable moments for everyone involved.

Location: Chennai
